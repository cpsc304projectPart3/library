    Borrower

    As a borrower, you can search for books on titles, authors and subjects. Also, you can check your account and pay fines. You can borrow books and place hold request on books.

    Clerk

    As a clerk, you can add borrowers and check for overdue books. Also, you can check out items for borrowers and process return items from borrowers.

    Librarian

    As a librarian, you can add a new book or a new copy of an existing book. Also, you can generate reports with all the books that have been checked out and the most popular books in a certain year.
